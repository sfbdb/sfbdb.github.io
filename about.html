What is "Super Fantasy Brawl"?

Super Fantasy Brawl is a fast-paced, competitive miniatures game. Players assemble a team from the greatest fantasy Champions and go head-to-head in an effort to become the winner of the Super Brawl!

Designed by Jochen Eisenhuth and published by Mythic Games

What is "Super Fantasy Brawl" DB?

This is an unofficial fan-made page listing all the cards that come in the Super Fantasy Brawl game. The card images displayed on this page are captured from Tabletopia and belong to Mythic Games.

Note that the manufacturing process of Super Fantasy Brawl is still in progress. The cards listed on this page, as captured from Tabletopia, are not 100% final.
